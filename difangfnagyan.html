Beijing Dialect, Exploring the Authentic Old Beijing Local Vernacular

Views: 57 times    Published: 2023-07-20

Speaking of Beijing dialect, it's quite interesting. Some of the old Beijing dialects are very attractive to some people. Our editor here at Shenyang Muyuan loves the old Beijing dialect very much. Many phrases are quite fascinating. Below, the editor has compiled some very authentic old Beijing dialects. Interested friends can take a look and learn together. The "erhua" sound, etc., are all very interesting!

1. "Liur zao er" (遛儿早儿): Take a walk in the morning. Example: Greet each other when meeting in the morning: "Going for a walk this morning?"

2. "Xie zhe ba" (歇着吧): (1) Means "take a rest." Example: "You take a rest, and I'll do it." (2) Means "stop it." Example: "You stop it, don't mess it up for me again."

3. "Dou jiling er" (抖机灵儿): Showing off oneself, especially in a frivolous manner, with a derogatory meaning. Example: (1) "Don't show off in front of me." Example (2) "Look, the new leader is here; he's going to show off again."
